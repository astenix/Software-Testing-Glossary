\section{Functional Testing}
\label{sec:Functional Testing}

\index{Functional Testing}Any testing based on an analysis of the specification of the functionality of a component or system.

The functional testing will be executed to evaluate the compliance of a system or component or third-party with specified functional \index{Requirements}requirements and corresponding predicted results.

Functional testing is performed for each planned feature and is guided by approved by \index{Customer}client requirements.
